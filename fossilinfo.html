Fossil Information

Florida has a rich history that allows for many kinds of fossils to be discovered from mammals of the Ice Age to the sharks of the sea. The following links provide additional information about fossils, Florida pre-history, and regulations.

Any fossil collecting on state owned lands in which vertebrate fossils are found, aside from sharks teeth, require a Fossil Permit. More about Fossil Permits and how they can be obtained can be found through this link to the Florida Museum of Natural History .
